The other TURN protocols is mainly to bypass restrictive firewalls or hide traffic. 

Then I want that the TURN server can support relaying traffic between two TURN clients.
This works like so: if a TURN client tries sending a UDP packet to a XOR-RELAYED-ADDRESS IP+port of another TURN client, then instead of sending
the UDP packet out, you just forward internally to the other TURN client. allowing TURN to TURN communication.
This is called short circuit optimalization. 

Then the second thing is that the TURN server can also be enabled to not allocate real ports, but only allow this
shortcut optimization (so internally if both clients speak TURN). In that case the XOR-RELAYED-ADDRESS can be fake (like use RFC local address space),
does not really matter. This is when internal routing only is enabled
